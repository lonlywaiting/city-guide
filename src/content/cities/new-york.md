## City Overview

New York is America's largest city, known as the "Capital of the World". From Wall Street's financial empire to Broadway's theatrical arts, from the Statue of Liberty to the Empire State Building, New York symbolizes dreams and opportunities.

## Top Attractions

- **Statue of Liberty** - American icon
- **Central Park** - City's green lung
- **Times Square** - Bustling center
- **Metropolitan Museum** - World-class museum

## Food Recommendations

New York Pizza, Hot Dogs, Bagels, cuisine from around the world.

## 城市简介

纽约是美国最大的城市，被誉为"世界之都"。从华尔街的金融帝国到百老汇的戏剧艺术，从自由女神像到帝国大厦，纽约是梦想与机遇的象征。

## 著名景点

- **自由女神像** - 美国象征
- **中央公园** - 城市绿肺
- **时代广场** - 繁华中心
- **大都会博物馆** - 世界级博物馆

## 美食推荐

纽约披萨、热狗、百吉饼、各国美食汇聚。
