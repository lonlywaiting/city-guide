## City Overview

Tokyo is Japan's capital and one of the world's largest metropolitan areas. This city perfectly blends modern technology with traditional culture, from high-tech Shibuya to historic Asakusa Temple, showcasing diverse charms.

## Top Attractions

- **Senso-ji Temple** - Tokyo's oldest temple
- **Tokyo Tower** - Iconic landmark
- **Imperial Palace** - Japanese Emperor's residence
- **Shibuya** - Bustling commercial district

## Food Recommendations

Sushi, Ramen, Tempura, Grilled Eel, and other traditional Japanese cuisine.

## 城市简介

东京是日本的首都，世界上最大的都市圈之一。这座城市完美融合了现代科技与传统文化，从高科技的涩谷到古朴的浅草寺，展现了多样的魅力。

## 著名景点

- **浅草寺** - 东京最古老的寺庙
- **东京塔** - 标志性建筑
- **皇居** - 日本天皇居住地
- **涩谷** - 繁华商业区

## 美食推荐

寿司、拉面、天妇罗、烤鳗鱼等日本传统美食。
