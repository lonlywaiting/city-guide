## City Overview

Vancouver is located on Canada's west coast, embraced by mountains and sea. This city perfectly blends urban life with natural beauty and has been repeatedly ranked as one of the world's most livable cities.

## Top Attractions

- **Stanley Park** - City's green lung
- **Capilano Suspension Bridge** - Rainforest experience
- **Granville Island** - Cultural market
- **Gastown** - Historic district

## Food Recommendations

Salmon, Seafood, Asian cuisine, Craft Beer.

## 城市简介

温哥华位于加拿大西海岸，被山海环抱。这座城市完美融合了都市生活与自然美景，多次被评为世界最宜居城市之一。

## 著名景点

- **斯坦利公园** - 城市绿肺
- **卡皮拉诺吊桥** - 雨林体验
- **格兰维尔岛** - 文化市集
- **煤气镇** - 历史街区

## 美食推荐

三文鱼、海鲜、亚洲美食、精酿啤酒。
