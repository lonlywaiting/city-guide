## City Overview

Edinburgh is the capital of Scotland, famous for its magnificent castle, rich history, and unique culture. The Old Town and New Town coexist, showcasing the city's development from the Middle Ages to the Enlightenment.

## Top Attractions

- **Edinburgh Castle** - Hilltop fortress
- **Royal Mile** - Historic street
- **Arthur's Seat** - Hill viewpoint
- **Calton Hill** - City panorama

## Food Recommendations

Scotch Whisky, Haggis, Scottish Salmon.

## 城市简介

爱丁堡是苏格兰首府，以其壮丽的城堡、丰富的历史和独特的文化而闻名。老城与新城并存，展现了这座城市从中世纪到启蒙时代的发展历程。

## 著名景点

- **爱丁堡城堡** - 制高点城堡
- **皇家英里大道** - 历史街道
- **亚瑟王座** - 山丘景观
- **卡尔顿山** - 城市全景

## 美食推荐

苏格兰威士忌、哈吉斯、苏格兰三文鱼。
