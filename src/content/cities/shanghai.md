## City Overview

Shanghai is China's largest economic center and international metropolis. The Bund's colonial architecture, Lujiazui's skyscrapers, and French Concession's plane trees showcase the city's unique East-meets-West charm.

## Top Attractions

- **The Bund** - Shanghai landmark
- **Oriental Pearl Tower** - Iconic building
- **Yu Garden** - Classical Chinese garden
- **Nanjing Road** - Bustling shopping street

## Food Recommendations

Xiaolongbao, Shengjianbao, Local Shanghai cuisine, Hairy Crab from Yangcheng Lake.

## 城市简介

上海是中国最大的经济中心和国际化大都市。外滩的万国建筑群、陆家嘴的摩天大楼、法租界的梧桐树，展现了这座城市中西合璧的独特魅力。

## 著名景点

- **外滩** - 上海地标
- **东方明珠** - 标志性建筑
- **豫园** - 江南古典园林
- **南京路** - 繁华商业街

## 美食推荐

小笼包、生煎包、本帮菜、阳澄湖大闸蟹。
