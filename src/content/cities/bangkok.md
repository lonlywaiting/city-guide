## City Overview

Bangkok is the capital of Thailand, a vibrant international metropolis. With its magnificent temples, modern shopping centers, bustling night markets, and delicious street food, this city offers unique charm.

## Top Attractions

- **Grand Palace** - Thai royal architecture
- **Wat Pho** - Famous Buddhist temple
- **Khao San Road** - Backpacker's paradise
- **Chao Phraya River** - City's lifeline

## Food Recommendations

Tom Yum Goong, Pad Thai, Mango Sticky Rice, Som Tam (Papaya Salad).

## 城市简介

曼谷是泰国的首都，充满活力的国际化都市。金碧辉煌的寺庙、现代化的购物中心、热闹的夜市和美味的街头小吃，构成了这座城市的独特魅力。

## 著名景点

- **大皇宫** - 泰国王室建筑
- **卧佛寺** - 著名佛教寺庙
- **考山路** - 背包客天堂
- **湄南河** - 城市母亲河

## 美食推荐

冬阴功汤、泰式炒河粉、芒果糯米饭、青木瓜沙拉。
