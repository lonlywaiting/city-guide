## City Overview

San Francisco is a jewel on the American West Coast and the center of technological innovation. The Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars, steep hills, and fog create this city's unique landscape. Neighbor to Silicon Valley, it symbolizes innovation and freedom.

## Top Attractions

- **Golden Gate Bridge** - Iconic landmark
- **Fisherman's Wharf** - Waterfront attraction
- **Alcatraz Island** - Historic prison
- **Lombard Street** - World's most crooked street

## Food Recommendations

Clam Chowder, Chinatown cuisine, Craft Beer.

## 城市简介

旧金山是美国西海岸的明珠，科技创新的中心。金门大桥、缆车、陡峭的山丘和浓雾，构成了这座城市的独特风景。硅谷的邻居，创新与自由精神的象征。

## 著名景点

- **金门大桥** - 标志性建筑
- **渔人码头** - 海滨景点
- **恶魔岛** - 历史监狱
- **九曲花街** - 世界最弯街道

## 美食推荐

海鲜浓汤、中国城美食、精酿啤酒。
