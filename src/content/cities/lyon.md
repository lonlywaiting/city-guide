## City Overview

Lyon is France's third-largest city, hailed as the "Culinary Capital". The Rhône and Saône rivers meet here, and Roman ruins, Renaissance architecture, and modern art coexist harmoniously in this city.

## Top Attractions

- **Old Town** - UNESCO World Heritage Site
- **Basilica of Notre-Dame de Fourvière** - City hilltop
- **Place Bellecour** - City center
- **Festival of Lights** - Annual event

## Food Recommendations

Lyon Sausage, Bresse Chicken, Cheese, Traditional Lyon Bouchons.

## 城市简介

里昂是法国第三大城市，被誉为"美食之都"。罗纳河与索恩河在此交汇，古罗马遗迹、文艺复兴建筑和现代艺术在这座城市和谐共存。

## 著名景点

- **老城区** - 世界文化遗产
- **富维耶圣母院** - 城市制高点
- **白莱果广场** - 市中心
- **灯光节** - 年度盛事

## 美食推荐

里昂香肠、布雷斯鸡、奶酪、里昂传统小餐馆。
