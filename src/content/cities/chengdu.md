## City Overview

Chengdu is the capital of Sichuan Province, known as the Land of Abundance. This city is famous for its leisurely lifestyle, spicy Sichuan cuisine, and adorable giant pandas.

## Top Attractions

- **Giant Panda Breeding Base** - See the national treasure
- **Kuanzhai Alley** - Traditional neighborhood  
- **Wuhou Shrine** - Three Kingdoms cultural site
- **Jinli** - Ancient-style commercial street

## Food Recommendations

Hot Pot, Chuan Chuan skewers, Mapo Tofu, Dan Dan Noodles, Long Chao Shou.

## 城市简介

成都是四川省会，被誉为"天府之国"。这座城市以悠闲的生活节奏、麻辣的川菜和可爱的大熊猫闻名。古蜀文明的发源地，现代化发展与历史文化完美融合。

## 著名景点

- **大熊猫繁育基地** - 观赏国宝
- **宽窄巷子** - 传统街区
- **武侯祠** - 三国文化圣地
- **锦里** - 古风商业街

## 美食推荐

火锅、串串香、麻婆豆腐、担担面、龙抄手。
