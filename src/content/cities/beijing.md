## City Overview

Beijing, the capital of China, has over 3,000 years of city history and 860 years as a capital. As China's political and cultural center, Beijing blends classical and modern elements, making it a charming international metropolis.

## Top Attractions

- **Forbidden City** - World's largest ancient palace complex
- **Great Wall** - One of the Seven Wonders of the World
- **Temple of Heaven** - Where Ming and Qing emperors worshipped heaven
- **Summer Palace** - China's largest surviving royal garden

## Food Recommendations

Peking Duck, Zhajiang Noodles, Douzhi, Hot Pot, and other traditional delicacies.

## 城市简介

北京，中国的首都，拥有3000多年的建城史和860多年的建都史。作为中国的政治、文化中心，北京融合了古典与现代，是一座充满魅力的国际化大都市。

## 著名景点

- **故宫** - 世界上最大的古代宫殿建筑群
- **长城** - 世界七大奇迹之一
- **天坛** - 明清两代皇帝祭天的场所
- **颐和园** - 中国现存最大的皇家园林

## 美食推荐

北京烤鸭、炸酱面、豆汁、涮羊肉等传统美食享誉中外。
