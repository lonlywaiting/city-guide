## City Overview

Paris, the capital and largest city of France, is known as the "City of Romance", "Fashion Capital", and "Art Capital". The beauty along the Seine, world-class museums and architecture make Paris one of the world's most popular tourist destinations.

## Top Attractions

- **Eiffel Tower** - Paris's iconic landmark
- **Louvre Museum** - World's largest art museum
- **Arc de Triomphe** - Monument to Napoleon's victories
- **Notre-Dame** - Gothic architectural masterpiece

## Food Recommendations

French Bread, Macarons, Foie Gras, Cheese, and other classic French cuisine.

## 城市简介

巴黎，法国的首都和最大城市，被誉为"浪漫之都"、"时尚之都"和"艺术之都"。塞纳河畔的美景、世界级的博物馆和建筑使巴黎成为全球最受欢迎的旅游目的地之一。

## 著名景点

- **埃菲尔铁塔** - 巴黎的标志性建筑
- **卢浮宫** - 世界最大的艺术博物馆
- **凯旋门** - 纪念拿破仑战争胜利
- **圣母院** - 哥特式建筑杰作

## 美食推荐

法式面包、马卡龙、鹅肝、奶酪等经典法式美食。
