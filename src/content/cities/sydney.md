## City Overview

Sydney is Australia's largest city, famous for its iconic Opera House and Harbour Bridge. Blue skies, azure seas, and golden beaches create this city's charming scenery, making it the most attractive international metropolis in the Southern Hemisphere.

## Top Attractions

- **Sydney Opera House** - UNESCO World Heritage Site
- **Harbour Bridge** - City landmark
- **Bondi Beach** - Surfing paradise
- **Darling Harbour** - Waterfront leisure area

## Food Recommendations

Seafood, Steak, Tim Tam chocolate biscuits, Australian Brunch.

## 城市简介

悉尼是澳大利亚最大的城市，以其标志性的歌剧院和海港大桥闻名于世。蓝天、碧海、金色沙滩构成了这座城市的迷人风景，是南半球最具魅力的国际都市。

## 著名景点

- **悉尼歌剧院** - 世界文化遗产
- **海港大桥** - 城市地标
- **邦迪海滩** - 冲浪天堂
- **达令港** - 海滨休闲区

## 美食推荐
