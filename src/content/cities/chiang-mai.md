## City Overview

Chiang Mai is a northern Thai city known as the "Rose of the North". Ancient temples, traditional handicrafts, peaceful atmosphere, and surrounding natural scenery make Chiang Mai an ideal place to relax.

## Top Attractions

- **Doi Suthep** - Hilltop temple
- **Old City** - Historical sites
- **Nimmanhaemin Road** - Artistic district
- **Night Market** - Local specialty

## Food Recommendations

Northern Thai Curry, Grilled Chicken, Mango Sticky Rice, Thai Milk Tea.

## 城市简介

清迈是泰国北部城市，被称为"泰北玫瑰"。古老的寺庙、传统的手工艺、宁静的氛围和周边的自然风光，使清迈成为放松心灵的理想之地。

## 著名景点

- **双龙寺** - 山顶寺庙
- **古城区** - 历史遗迹
- **宁曼路** - 文艺街区
- **夜市** - 当地特色

## 美食推荐

泰北咖喱、烤鸡、芒果饭、泰式奶茶。
