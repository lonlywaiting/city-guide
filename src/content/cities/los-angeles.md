Los Angeles is America's second-largest city and the center of the world's entertainment industry. Hollywood glamour, Malibu beaches, Beverly Hills luxury - LA is synonymous with dreams and opportunities.

洛杉矶是美国第二大城市，世界娱乐产业的中心。好莱坞的星光、马里布的海滩、比佛利山庄的奢华，洛杉矶是梦想与机遇的代名词。
